About Root

An external perceptual, internal rational gamification consumption management tool.

Target User

Exquisite and poor.

Refers to young people who are not making much money but spend a lot of money. In order to buy something they like, even if they are too poor, they feel that they live a delicate and shiny life. The most important manifestation is: consumption that does not match the receipts.

Who?

18-30 years old/prone to impulsive consumption/monthly deposit ratio is less than 20%/first- and second-tier urban youth.

what?

It is convenient to choose between functions and services, and make more stylized accounting software.

how?

Scientific and flexible consumption management model + gamification persuasion mechanism.
